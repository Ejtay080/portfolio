What is West-MEC?

Western Maricopa Education Center (West-MEC) is a public school district which provides career training programs for both high school and adult students in Arizona. West-MEC focuses solely on innovative career and technical education programs that prepare students to enter the workforce and pursue continuing education.

There is three types of education available from West-MEC

  • High School Student Programs
  • Adult Education Programs
  • Professional Development Programs

What types of Programs?

There are two types of programs in a High School Student Education.

  • West-MEC Central Programs
  • West-MEC gives high school students a faster way forward through career and technical education (CTE) courses. Students spend part of their day learning their core subjects at a public, charter, private, or home school, then take their career training program at a West-MEC or partner campus.
  • West-MEC Satellite Programs
  • You may be taking a West-MEC course and not even know it! West-MEC provides funding to our Member School Districts to teach career and technical education course. If you attend any of the following high schools and are taking a CTE course, it could be a West-MEC Satellite Program. To find out more about West-MEC and the opportunities available to you, contact your school counselor today!

There is one type of program in a Adult Education.

  • Career Training Programs for Adult Students
  • Certifications and licensure open the doors to economic independence and a fulfilling, in-demand career. For questions about enrollment, please contact 623.738.0046.

There are many types of programs in Professional Development.

  • West-MEC offers a series of professional development courses and workshops available to anyone interested in increasing their skill set. Whether you are in education or the private sector, there is a professional development workshop available to you. All participants will receive a certificate documenting their hours of participation for recertification purposes.
